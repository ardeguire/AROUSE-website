1. Go to Nicecast
  2. Start archiving:
    1. Go to "Control" menu.
    2. Click "Start Archiving".
  3. Do your talking or whatever it is you want to record.
  4. Stop archiving:
    1. Go to "Control" menu.
    2. Click "Stop Archiving".
  5. Files are stored in the folder: ~/Music/Nicecast Broadcast Archive as "Nicecast Archived Radio yyyymmdd hhmm" with hours as 24h clock.
  6. Pick the one you recorded and put it on a flash drive or e-mail it to yourself or something.

Note: as a shortcut, you can hit Command-R to toggle archiving when Nicecast is the active application.
